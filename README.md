### 🧠 What I'm building

| Project | What it does |
|---|---|
| 🛡️ [hallucination-firewall](https://github.com/zain-the-npc/hallucination-firewall) | A real-time pipeline that scores LLM answers for hallucination risk using a custom fine-tuned DistilBERT model, cross-verifies with a second LLM, and auto-corrects flagged answers with RAG |
| 🔍 [Fake-News-Detector-using-RAG-](https://github.com/zain-the-npc/Fake-News-Detector-using-RAG-) | A fact-checker that grounds its verdicts in the LIAR dataset instead of just asking an LLM to guess |
| 🧩 [SynapseAI](https://github.com/zain-the-npc/SynapseAI) | A multimodal study companion — feed it a PDF, PPT, or photo of your notes, get back quizzes, flashcards, or a plain-language explanation |
| 🌦️ [Mausam](https://github.com/zain-the-npc/Mausam) | A weather app that pairs live conditions with mood-matched quotes and ambient sound |
